Rules

Your main mission is to gather food items to raise your score without bringing your weight over 500kg. Every item also has an effect on your weight, for example burger will make your pig fatter and cherries will make your pig lose some weight.

Values:

= Score + 150 & Weight - 20

= Score + 250 & Weight + 20

= Score + 500 & Weight + 30

= unable to gain or lose weight for 5 seconds.
